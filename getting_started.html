Let's get going.

Being the sole employee on the planet is going to be slow going at, at first. It's important to understand what you should be doing.

Scan for resources

Iron ore will be the lifeblood of your future factory. You will want to scan for its location.
By holding V and selecting iron ore, you will be able to quickly locate and travel to this valuable resource.

Gather Ore

Now that you've moved your legs the required number of times to reach the ore you must now collect it. You are sure to reach your daily 10,000 steps!
By tapping E you will will be able to chisel away and collect the ore.

Build your Habitat and Utility Base (HUB)

This will act as your home, base, and place of work. This isn't a cubicle; it's a work from home opportunity.
By pressing Q you will will be able to select and place your HUB. Be sure to allow it some space.

Build your first Iron ingot!

You may not be a talented blacksmith, but with FICSIT technology even you can create a metal brick!
After approaching your workbench press E select the item you want to craft, Ingot, and then tap E.


There is still so much more to do. But it's important to get acquainted with your factory's building blocks.
